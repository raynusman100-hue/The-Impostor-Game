# Download Sound Effects

Download these free sounds from Pixabay and save them to `assets/sounds/`:

## Required Files

1. **tap.mp3** - Button click sound
   - Go to: https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/ui%20click/
   - Download any short click sound
   - Rename to `tap.mp3`

2. **success.mp3** - Win/success sound  
   - Go to: https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/success/
   - Download a short success jingle
   - Rename to `success.mp3`

3. **error.mp3** - Error/wrong sound
   - Go to: https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/error/
   - Download a short error buzz
   - Rename to `error.mp3`

4. **reveal.mp3** - Card flip/reveal sound
   - Go to: https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/whoosh/
   - Download a short whoosh sound
   - Rename to `reveal.mp3`

5. **timer.mp3** - Timer beep
   - Go to: https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/beep/
   - Download a short beep
   - Rename to `timer.mp3`

6. **vote.mp3** - Vote/select sound
   - Go to: https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/pop/
   - Download a short pop sound
   - Rename to `vote.mp3`
